3D ANIMATED MUSIC VIDEO FOR AXOLOTES MEXICANOS
The piece, commisioned by the popular Spanish band Axolotes Mexicanos, tells a whimsical story about a girl who falls in love with a nonchalant octopus from outer space and her obsessive, one-sided quest to win its affection. The narrative explores themes of desire and emotional vulnerability through a playful, cute lens, blending dark undertones with charming humor. Visually, the project draws inspiration from classic Nintendo and Sega aesthetics, combining colorful, game-like imagery with expressive animation.

storyboard FOR THE MUSIC VIDEO

3D CHARACTER DESIGN FOR A VIDEOGAME
This character was designed and modeled around the concept of an elderly boxer. The proposal portrays a former aristocrat brought to ruin by his own greed, forced to turn to underground street boxing as a last resort. The character was fully sculpted, hand-painted, and optimized for videogame use, with a strong focus on silhouette, texture readability, and in-game performance.
EXPERIMENTAL INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE DESIGN
This immersive journey explores the fragile edges of consciousness and memory in the afterlife, blending photogrammetry with procedural deformation to create a space that feels both intimately known and deeply wrong. After dying with no extra lives left, you drift through the places you once visited most as your memories unravel, the world distorts, the music decays into static, and reality slowly becomes unrecognizable.
This interactive experience was developed in Unity and the environments were created using Blender and photogrammetry techniques.
